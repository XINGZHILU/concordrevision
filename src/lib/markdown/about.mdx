# Our project
_Our team members:Xingzhi Lu, Joshua Ng, Christoph Chan_

Congratulations on pressing random buttons and finding this subpage!
This website is desinged for intellectual exchange between students, where students and teachers can upload their notes, resources and practice questions.

### Why did we start this project?
> It all started with a study group.

From the success of the school as a collective received the highest ever GCSE grades(90% 9/8/7) in 2024, we understand that besides brilliant individual performances, the teamwork and the spirit of sharing and caring is what got us over the line. We treasure every moment of interaction with peers and the happiness studying together brings.

### Our beliefs
>Support is needed when you need it. We are in this together.

'Be kind to others, but also be kind to yourself.''
: We hope to create a collaborative environment where everyone can be recognised for their impact on their peers and their friends—even something as simple as just being there.
We are here to offer guidance on studying and organising your time efficiently if you don't know where to start! We understand that studying in a different system coming from different countries may be difficult to start with, so we provide examplar notes and tips for exhaustive subjects and olympiads.

There are no shortcuts.
: Actual revision, however, is individual work done by yourself. This website should just be used as a reference and in addition to your notes. We encourage writing notes in your way because simply we all learn differently and it will be more memorable.


### Future aspirations

We hope this website acts not only for its academic purpose but also as something that passes on the communal nature of Concord - leaving no one behind.
